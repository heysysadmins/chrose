    Instructions

  1. Powerwash by attempting to enable Developer Mode. You may want to back up important local data on a usb or a Google Drive
  2. Connect to wifi in quick settings (shift+alt+s)
  3. Press ctrl+alt+q and get started
  4. Transfer wifi from phone
  5. Connect your phone from the popup
  6. Scan the QR and do the fingerprint
  7. Transfer wifi from phone again
  8. Close app on phone
  9. Log into Google account
  10. Do Chromebook setup
  11. Press ctrl+shift+q+q
  12. Press get started
  13. Press next
  14. You should be greeted by three options, choose the first one and sign in to your home account
