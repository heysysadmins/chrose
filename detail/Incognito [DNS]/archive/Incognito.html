This exploit makes use of a silly mistake recently introduced into ChromeOS. It allows students to bypass extensions and browse in incognito by doing some trickery with obscure types of windows. Your history will not be recorded while you are in incognito.

After the incognito window has opened, it is recommended to switch your DNS servers back to manual, then disconnect and reconnect to your Wi-Fi to get faster loading. The incognito window will stay open until your browser or computer restarts, or you close it.
