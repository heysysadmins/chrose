Chrome://chrome-signin Webview

the url chrome://chrome-signin is blocked for a lot of people or shows a blank screen

Details

    Instructions

  1. Go to chrome://chrome-signin.
  2. Click OK on the bottom right corner.
  3. In the email text box put google@d11.org
  4. Click Sign-in options.
  5. Click Sign-in with Github.
  6. Click Forgot password?
  7. Click the cat icon at the top
  8. In the search box at the top right, type Google.
  9. Click Search all of Github.
  10. Click the Google link in the infobox about Google to the right of the screen.
  11. You are in Google and all websites are unblocked!
  12. As there are no tabs, use the forward and back keys on the top row.

    The Github secondary rate limit:

    If you go on GitHub enough, you will receive a secondary rate limit. Luckily, there is a way to mitigate this.

  1. When you finish step 7 from above, click on the little thing that says @githubstatus, or any other elon musk site thing that may happen to appear.
  2. On Twitter, click sign in with apple.
  3. Click on the Apple logo.
  4. Search up Youtube in the apple search bar.
  5. Scroll down and click "visit developer site".
  6. You can stop here if you're a nerd, or you can click on the youtube settings/widget bar and click on the terms of service.
  7. Click on the google license link at the bottom.
  8. You are in Google and all websites are unblocked!
